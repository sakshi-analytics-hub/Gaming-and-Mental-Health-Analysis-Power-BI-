🎮 Gaming and Mental Health Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between gaming behavior and mental health using data from 1000 participants. It explores how gaming habits impact sleep, academic performance, productivity, and social isolation.

The analysis is visualized using Power BI dashboards to uncover patterns and behavioral insights.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze gaming patterns across users
Identify gaming addiction risk levels
Study the impact of gaming on sleep quality
Evaluate effects on GPA and work productivity
Examine social isolation trends
📊 Key Insights Covered
Gaming activity across different age groups and genders
Gaming habits and platform preferences
Gaming addiction risk classification
Impact on academic and work performance
Relationship between gaming, sleep, and productivity
Behavioral patterns based on genre and experience
🧾 Dataset
Total Participants: 1000
Includes data on:
Daily gaming hours
Sleep hours
GPA
Work productivity
Gaming platform and genre
Addiction risk levels
🛠 Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Data Visualization
Data Analysis
Statistical Correlation
📈 Key Metrics
Average Daily Gaming Hours: 6.15 hours
Average Sleep Hours: 5.74 hours
Average GPA: ~2.18
Social Isolation Score: 3.87
High Risk Users Identified: 154
🔍 Findings
Higher gaming hours are associated with reduced sleep quality
Increased gaming time correlates with lower GPA and productivity
Users with severe addiction show higher social isolation
Gaming genre and platform influence addiction risk
Long-term gaming experience can increase behavioral risks
📊 Visualizations

The Power BI dashboard includes:

Gaming activity analysis

Academic and productivity impact

🚀 Conclusion

The analysis highlights the significant impact of gaming behavior on mental health and lifestyle factors. The findings can help in understanding gaming addiction patterns and improving awareness regarding healthy gaming habits.
